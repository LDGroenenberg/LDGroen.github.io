<How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ/>

1. Select the following printer for your job: HZ Printer.
2. When the print job is given, log in using the HZ pass or by entering your HZ username & password.
3. Then choose the Print Release option. You will then see which print jobs are ready.
   If there is sufficient balance in your print account, the print jobs will be printed.
   Tap Access Device to see your balance. After printing, tap the following icon on the printer to log out.

<How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ/>

1. Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your HZ user name & password.
2. Choose the Scan option in the menu.
3. Place the original(s) on the feeder or glass plate.
4. Double-sided scanning (optional).
 • Press Change settings.
 • For duplex mode, select the 2-sided option.
5. Press Start. 6. After scanning, press the right top symbol on the printer to log off.

<How can you book a project space/>

1. Go to the home page of the Self Service Portal.
2. Click on Reservations.
3. Choose the appropriate category and search for the "resource" you want to reserve.
   If necessary, use the Type filter to turn certain items on/off in the overview.
4. Select the day when the resource is borrowed
5. By dragging the left mouse button, indicate the time period when the resource is borrowed.
6. Click Next.
7. Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click Next.
8. Fill in the necessary information and click Submit request.

<What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot/>

It is not possible to park directly at the HZ on Het Groene Woud.
There is 1 special parking space available on the grounds for people with disabilities
and/or chronic conditions.

The car park on Kousteensedijk has a number of spaces for visitors,
staff and students of Het Groene Woud.
This car park is an 8-minute walk from the HZ. Upon showing your entry card,
you can then get an exit card at the JRCZ service desk.
